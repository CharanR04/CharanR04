Selected projects spanning time-series forecasting, multimodal AI, and computer vision.

Remote Sensing & Monitoring Soil Moisture

Designed an ESP32-based IoT system to monitor soil moisture and maintain it within safe ranges, helping to protect structural foundations. The system logs data for analysis and integrates with control logic for automated actuation.

Water Supply & Demand Forecasting

Built LSTM-based models to forecast water supply and demand patterns. Analyzed seasonality, consumption behaviour, and anomalies; visualized results in a dashboard to support planning and decision-making.

Visual Bot – Multimodal VQA for Accessibility

Developed a pipeline that takes an image and a natural language query, then returns context-aware answers. Explored transformer-based VQA architectures and transfer learning (ULMFiT-style ideas) for domain adaptation.

Layer Height Monitoring in 3D Printing

Used computer vision to detect anomalies in 3D printer layer heights. Built a deep learning model to classify defects using images captured during the printing process, improving reliability of the additive manufacturing workflow.
