About Me

I'm a final-year B.Tech student in Computer Science (AI & ML) at Chennai Institute of Technology, with a CGPA of 8.09/10.

My research spans Multimodal AI, Visual Question Answering, stock market forecasting, IoT-integrated systems, and AI for additive manufacturing. I've had the privilege of conducting research at NIT Tiruchirappalli and Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman, Malaysia.
