About Me

I work at the intersection of deep learning, multimodal AI, and interpretable models.

I am pursuing a B.E. in Computer Science (AI & ML) at Chennai Institute of Technology. My work focuses on computer vision, natural language processing, and time-series forecasting, with an interest in models that can explain their decisions.

Through research internships at NIT Tiruchirappalli and UTAR Malaysia, and work at the Center for Additive Manufacturing, I’ve contributed to projects in Visual Question Answering, forecasting, and AI for manufacturing.
