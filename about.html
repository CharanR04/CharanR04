About Me

Computer Science (AI & ML) undergraduate from Chennai, building towards a research career in interpretable and multimodal AI.

I am currently pursuing my B.E. in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) at Chennai Institute of Technology. My work so far has centred on computer vision, natural language processing, and time-series forecasting, with a strong emphasis on how models can be made more interpretable and practically useful.

Through research internships at NIT Tiruchirappalli and UTAR Malaysia, as well as ongoing work at my institute’s Center for Additive Manufacturing, I’ve had the opportunity to explore multimodal VQA systems, forecasting architectures, and AI-assisted manufacturing workflows.

I enjoy environments where theory, experimentation, and engineering come together: designing architectures, analysing model behaviour, and thinking about how these systems can support human decision-making in a transparent way.
