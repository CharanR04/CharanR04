About Me

Computer Science (AI & ML) undergraduate from Chennai, building towards a research career in interpretable and multimodal AI.

I'm currently pursuing my B.E. in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) at Chennai Institute of Technology. Over the last few years, I’ve worked across computer vision, natural language processing, and time-series forecasting, mainly through research internships and lab projects.

I’m particularly drawn to problems where human intuition, domain knowledge, and data-driven models have to work together: forecasting complex systems, interpreting model decisions, and designing interfaces that make AI more usable.

Outside of research, I enjoy exploring deep learning frameworks, tinkering with devices like Raspberry Pi / ESP32, improving my math and physics foundations, and slowly getting better at guitar.
